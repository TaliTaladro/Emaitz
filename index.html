What EMAITZ Does

EMAITZ provides advanced engineering modeling and analysis services focused on energy efficiency, wind energy, and complex flow systems. The work is grounded in first-principles physics and high-fidelity numerical simulation.

  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)
  • Wind energy and aerodynamic analysis
  • Energy system modeling and efficiency assessment
  • Thermal systems and heat transfer
  • Performance analysis and optimization

Approach

  1. Problem definition — clear understanding of the engineering question
  2. Model development — appropriate physical models and numerical methods
  3. Simulation and analysis — validated, high-quality simulations
  4. Results and insight — quantitative outputs translated into decisions

Expertise

EMAITZ is led by Ivan Torrano Zabalza, an engineer with experience in CFD, energy systems, and thermal–fluid modeling. The background combines academic rigor with applied industrial work.

Why EMAITZ

  • Physics-based, not black-box modeling
  • Clear communication of assumptions and limitations
  • Focus on measurable engineering results
  • Independent and software-agnostic approach
